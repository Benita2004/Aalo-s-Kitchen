Welcome to Aromas of Africa

About Me

Passion for Culture & Tech: Hi, I’m Benita Aalo, a Computer Science student with a love for technology and the flavours of home.

I created Aromas of Africa as a digital space where international students can reconnect with their roots through food. It’s a celebration of culture, comfort, and community where every dish tells a story.

I believe that food and technology both bring people together, and through projects like this I’m exploring how digital tools can make cultural experiences more accessible and heartwarming.
