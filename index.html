👩🏽‍💻 About Me

Hi, I’m Benita Aalo a Computer Science student passionate about blending culture and technology. I created Aalo’s Kitchen as a creative project to help international students connect with home through food. It’s where tech meets taste — and where every dish tells a story.

I believe that food and technology both bring people together, and through projects like this, I’m exploring how digital tools can make cultural experiences more accessible, engaging, and heartwarming.
