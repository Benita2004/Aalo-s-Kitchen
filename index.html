Welcome to Aromas of Africa

As an African international student, I know how important it is to find food that feels like home. That’s why I created Aromas of Africa a space where flavour meets comfort. Discover authentic African restaurants and dishes from Nigeria, Ghana, Somalia, and South Africa that celebrate culture and connection in every bite. Understanding the importance of limiting language barriers i included different languages for each country listed in the website. Enjoy your mealllll.

About Me

Passion for Culture & Tech: Hi, I’m Benita Aalo, a Computer Science student who loves combining creativity with technology.

I created Aromas of Africa as a digital space for international students to reconnect with their roots through food. It’s a celebration of culture, comfort, and community where every dish tells a story.

I believe food and technology both bring people together. Through projects like this, I’m exploring how digital tools can make cultural experiences more accessible and heartwarming.
